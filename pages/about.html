Our Story & Mission

From a small nursery school to a holistic hub for education, wellness, and innovation.

How It Started

2017: Tubafashe Academy School was established with an ambitious goal: to protect local children from abuse and violence and provide them with qualitative education.


2018: We opened the Nursery School with only 14 students. By the second semester, we had grown to 36 students, offering free education and meals to vulnerable families.


Today: We have evolved into the Creative Learning & Wellness Harmony Hub. We now serve the community through four pillars: Education, Mental Wellness, Sustainable Farming, and Arts.

Leadership

Dr. Uwihoreye Chaste

Founder & Director


Dr. Chaste is the visionary behind the Hub. A clinical psychologist with a Master’s degree from the University of Rwanda, he has over 15 years of experience working with traumatized children and community development.


He serves on the board of various national and international organizations and believes that a school is the best place to prevent child abuse and build a safe future.


"Sending children to school is the key investment for humanity."
