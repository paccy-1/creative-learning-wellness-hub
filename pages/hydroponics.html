Cornucopia Hydroponics

Promoting sustainable nutrition and food security through innovative soilless farming.

Visit The Farm

We teach children and families modern techniques for growing nutritious food without soil. By fostering environmental awareness, we are building a future of healthy eating habits and sustainable living.

Why Hydroponics?

Water Efficient

Hydroponics uses up to 90% less water than traditional agriculture, making it perfect for our environment and climate resilience.

Better Nutrition

We grow leafy greens and vegetables rich in vitamins to combat malnutrition in our student body and community.

Education

Our farm is a living classroom. Students learn biology, chemistry, and agri-business skills by managing the crops.
